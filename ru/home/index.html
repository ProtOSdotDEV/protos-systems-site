// about

What is this?

ProtOS Systems is a hub of projects the ProtOS developers are, have, or are planning to work on. Our main focus is on our own operating system, ProtOS. Throughout our time from starting ProtOS in 2025, to now, We've landed on a few more projects that have to do with Windows, Linux, macOS, and even some hardware projects, such as installing Linux on MacBook Silicon, hardware for protogens, and more. This website will be frequently updated with new developer logs, project status updates and even posts about our work.

Why do you care? If you've landed here, either you're unreasonably lost, or you take interest in low-level systems or free and open source software. Whether you're a protogen looking to get a visor, or a tech nerd who's looking to get Linux on their MacBook, this is where you would come to get that.
