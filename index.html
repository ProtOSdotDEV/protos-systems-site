What is this?

ProtOS Systems is a hub of projects the ProtOS Developers are, have, or are planning to work on. Our main focus is on our own operating system, ProtOS. Through our time from starting ProtOS in 2025, to now, We've landed on a few more projects, That have to do with Wndows, Linux, and even some hardware projects. Such as, installingn Linux on MacBook Silicon, hardware for protogens, and more. This website will be frequently updated with new Developer logs, project status updates and even posts about our work. Why do you care? If you landed here, either you're unreasonable lost, or you take interest into low-level systems, free and open source software and you found yourself here, whether you are a protogen looking to get a Visor, a tech nerd who's looking to get linux on their MacBook, then this is where you would come to get that.
